Creating Opportunities for Youth

ASKINA Foundation connects underprivileged students with job opportunities, scholarships, and safe housing through partnerships with local businesses and organizations.


About Us

At ASKINA Foundation, we believe every child deserves a chance—regardless of their background. Our mission is rooted in compassion, equity, and the unwavering belief that opportunity should never be a privilege, but a right. We are dedicated to supporting children and young people from socially vulnerable families by opening doors they never thought possible. Through our growing network of partners—employers, educators, and housing organizations—we help them find stable job opportunities, secure scholarships, enroll in universities, and access safe and affordable student housing. For many of these children, we are not just a stepping stone, but a lifeline. We stand by them from their first job interview to their first day at university—ensuring that their dreams don’t fade because of circumstances beyond their control. This is not charity. This is justice. And we are committed to making it real, one young life at a time.


What does ASKINA stand for?

A – Access to Opportunities
We provide access to education, jobs, and housing for underprivileged youth.

S – Support for Students
Continuous support to help students reach their full potential.

K – Knowledge Empowerment
We empower young people through knowledge, training, and mentorship.

I – Inclusion & Equity
Promoting inclusion and fair chances for everyone, regardless of their background.

N – Network of Partnerships
Building a strong network with businesses, educational institutions, and communities.

Scholarships

Education should never be a luxury—it should be a promise. At ASKINA Foundation, we are turning that promise into reality for young people who need it the most.

Our scholarship program is designed to uplift students from socially vulnerable backgrounds by removing the financial barriers that stand between them and their dreams. Whether it's tuition fees, books, application costs, or transportation—our support goes beyond just financial aid. We invest in the full journey of each student.

We work closely with universities, donors, and local communities to identify young talents with strong potential and limited means. Through our scholarships, these students are not just given an opportunity to study—they are given hope, dignity, and a future they can shape on their own terms.

Every scholarship we award is a commitment—to stand beside these students, encourage their growth, and help them break the cycle of poverty through education.

Because we believe that a single degree can lift an entire family—and change the course of a community.
